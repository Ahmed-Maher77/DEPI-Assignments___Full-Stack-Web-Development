About DEPI

The Digital Egypt Pioneers Initiative (DEPI) is a national program launched by the Egyptian Ministry of Communications and Information Technology (MCIT) to empower youth with cutting-edge skills in full-stack web development, software engineering, data science, AI, and more. DEPI provides high-quality training through both online and in-person sessions to prepare participants for real-world tech careers.

Learn more on the official platform: www.depi.gov.eg

Objectives

  • Equip participants with practical, job-ready skills in web development.
  • Bridge the gap between academic study and the software industry.
  • Promote digital transformation and technical innovation in Egypt.
  • Foster strong foundations in teamwork, UI/UX, testing, and deployment.
